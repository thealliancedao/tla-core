`[solid.stablecoin]`); **CAPA** is the governance/utility token `[solid.capa_governance]`; **ampCAPA**
is the Eris-amplified CAPA LST (ratio binds live `[solid.ampcapa_hub_ratio]`). Also operates the
**Atrium** NFT marketplace and DeFi games `[solid.atrium_marketplace]`.
